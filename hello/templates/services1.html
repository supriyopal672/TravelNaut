ChinaTown,Kolkata
The traditional occupation of the Chinese Indian community in Kolkata had been working in the nearby tanning industry as well as in Chinese restaurants. The area is still noted for the Chinese restaurants where many people flock to taste traditional Chinese and Indian Chinese cuisine.

OG By The Lake
A panoramic view of a waterbody, the Maa flyover, and a vibe that matches the view, OG By The Lake is the expansive (16,000sq ft) new cafe-cum-dine den that opens doors today. Nestled in the corner plot at Calcutta Boating and Hotel Resorts, right next to the play turf Orchid Arena, is this 250-seater space that has an indoor air-conditioned glass house, and numerous semi-open and outdoor decks for guests to soak in the best of the city, alongside some decadent world cuisine.
